Bio

I'm a year 4 undergraduate student at the University of Hong Kong (HKU), majoring in Computer Engineering in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE). I'm fortunate to work with the HKU MedAI Lab, supervised by Prof. Lequan Yu and HKU InfobodiedAI Lab, supervised by Prof. Yanchao Yang. I also spent a one-semester exchange at the Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in Fall 2024, Colledge of Computing and Data Science (CCDS), where I had the opportunity to collaborate with Prof. Bo An's group.

My research interests lie in both the AI for healthcare (especially LLM for healthcare) and embodied AI. Currently, I'm working on projects related to Human-Scene/Human-Object Interaction.
